Our Platform

Three Engines. One Intelligent SpatialOps Platform.

InventoryVisionHub combines computer vision intelligence, warehouse-state interpretation, and predictive operational analytics into one unified platform transforming inventory management from a manual operational burden into a measurable driver of efficiency, visibility, and margin improvement.

Engine 01

Vision-to-Operational State Engine

Converts raw warehouse imagery into structured operational intelligence identifying misplaced stock, overloaded zones, empty-but-assigned storage areas, and inventory inconsistencies in real time.

Powered by the Warehouse Visual Ontology (WVO), the engine transforms camera feeds into structured spatial objects while preserving warehouse-specific operational context across pallet racks, narrow aisles, tote systems, and bulk-floor environments.
Engine 02

Discrepancy Delta Diagnostics (DDD)

Analyses the difference between expected inventory records and actual warehouse conditions to identify the operational root causes behind inventory drift and fulfilment inaccuracies.

The engine detects occupied-but-unrecorded locations, receiving inconsistencies, and recurring pick-path friction while generating structured audit-ready discrepancy intelligence reports aligned with operational compliance requirements.
Engine 03

Community Waste Intelligence Network

Maps real-time warehouse movement signals from forklifts, pallets, totes, and material-handling operations to create continuous operational visibility across inventory zones.

The platform automatically generates structured discrepancy intelligence reports whenever movement patterns indicate abnormal inventory behaviour. Performance-Weighted Cycle Count Evolution ensures high-risk warehouse zones are prioritised for continuous refinement and operational optimisation.

The Platform

Every Layer of InventoryVisionHub's Warehouse Intelligence

InventoryVisionHub is a cloud-native SaaS platform built on a modular architecture real-time dashboards, MHE camera integration, proprietary spatial logic models, and UK GDPR-compliant infrastructure designed to serve UK mid-market 3PLs without requiring internal engineering teams.

Module 01

Warehouse Visual Ontology (WVO)

Machine learning models that identify and "link" detected objects (pallets, totes, cartons) to precise spatial coordinates, allowing benchmarking and cloning of optimal storage logic across diverse facility footprints.

Each warehouse develops a unique "Spatial DNA" a combination of aisle-bay-rack coordinates, stacking irregularities, and occupancy velocity profiles. This methodology is proprietary and creates defensible, audit-ready institutional warehouse intelligence.
Module 02

Discrepancy Delta Diagnostics

A proprietary diagnostic engine that automatically maps the root causes of inventory drift high-velocity pick errors, receiving-point friction, ghost inventory into structured forensic intelligence reports.

Trained on UK-specific warehouse patterns, pallet profiles, and HSE racking guidelines. Provides hyper-localised predictive power unavailable to generic barcode-heavy WMS systems detecting discrepancy risk before a shipping error occurs.
Module 03

Passive Audit Infrastructure

Integrates with fixed cameras, forklift-mounted webcams, and MHE telemetry to enrich the spatial model with real-time operational data across all warehouse zones without halting operations.

Asset-agnostic architecture supporting CCTV, smartphones, and reach truck vision units. Warehouses aren't locked into a single hardware vendor the platform sits above all camera systems with Sensor Fusion Autocalibration (SFAP).
Module 04

Signal-to-Action Auto-Synthesis

Automatically deconstructs a single visual signal a blocked aisle or an empty-but-assigned rack into a complex, multi-step operational workflow, removing manual staff-to-data translation bottlenecks.

Validated to demonstrate 25–40% increase in audit speed via automated discrepancy path synthesis. Risk-Weighted Cycle Count Evolution ensures high-risk zones are automatically prioritised and refined in real time.
Module 05

Community Waste Intelligence Network

A federated learning architecture enabling anonymised "Accuracy DNA" benchmarks from UK warehouses to continuously improve predictive models without any facility sharing sensitive commercial data.

Insights from high-efficiency Midlands fulfilment centres can optimise audit strategies for new Northern Powerhouse facilities. This collective intelligence ecosystem creates a network effect that new players would have to start from scratch to build.
Module 06

Outcome-Learning Warehouse Memory

Closes the loop linking every successful discrepancy resolution to operational outcomes and automatically preserving the "Institutional Spatial DNA" of experienced warehouse supervisors.

The Human-in-the-Loop Stewardship Index ensures that while the platform automates discrepancy detection, a human validation layer remains for high-value inventory exceptions satisfying strict UK logistics audit requirements. New staff reach proficiency in 14 days vs 45 days historically.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know About InventoryVisionHub

What is the "Visibility Gap" that InventoryVisionHub addresses?

+
The Visibility Gap is the critical disconnect between UK warehouse physical reality and digital WMS records. Currently, most mid-market 3PLs rely on infrequent manual cycle counts and reactive handheld scanning meaning 1 in 10 items is effectively "invisible" to the Warehouse Management System. InventoryVisionHub's Vision-to-Operational State Engine closes this gap through automated computer vision and passive spatial intelligence.

What is "Spatial DNA" and why does it matter for my warehouse?

+
Spatial DNA is the unique fingerprint of your warehouse's operational logic the specific aisle-bay-rack configurations, pick-frequency patterns, overflow thresholds, and storage slot assignments that govern how inventory should flow. By capturing and storing this intelligence, InventoryVisionHub ensures your warehouse's expert operational knowledge remains intact through staff turnover and operational changes, making it a permanent business asset rather than personal knowledge.

Which camera systems does InventoryVisionHub integrate with?

+
InventoryVisionHub is designed with an asset-agnostic architecture that sits above any underlying camera system. Current integrations support fixed CCTV cameras, forklift-mounted webcams, smartphones, and MHE telemetry units. Our Sensor Fusion Autocalibration (SFAP) ensures consistent data quality regardless of camera type, angle, or lighting conditions so warehouses are not locked into any single hardware vendor.

What results have pilot warehouses seen?

+
Across 8-week pilots in London, Manchester, and Birmingham, participating locations saw: a 28% average increase in audit speed (ICQA compliance) through automated discrepancy-path synthesis; an 18% reduction in physical-to-digital mismatch events across 1,200 tested pallet and tote locations; and new warehouse hires reaching audit proficiency within 14 days versus a historical average of 45 days. User satisfaction was rated 4.6/5 for "Institutional Knowledge Protection."

How does InventoryVisionHub protect inventory accuracy during automated audits?

+
Accuracy protection is core to the platform's design. The Discrepancy Delta Diagnostics (DDD) engine monitors all inventory zones in real-time and alerts operators before a mismatch causes a shipment failure. Human-in-the-Loop validation is retained for high-value or high-risk inventory exceptions, ensuring automated intelligence is always governed by experienced warehouse judgement.

Is InventoryVisionHub aligned with UK regulatory requirements?

+
Yes. InventoryVisionHub is strategically designed to align with the UK's "Pro-innovation approach to AI regulation" set by DSIT, adhering to the five core principles: Safety, Security & Resilience; Appropriate Transparency & Explainability; Fairness; Accountability & Governance; and Contestability & Redress. The platform also maintains strict UK GDPR compliance for operational imagery processing and follows ICO guidance on AI and Data Protection.

What is the Community Waste Intelligence Network (C-WIN)?

+
C-WIN is InventoryVisionHub's federated learning architecture that allows anonymised "Accuracy DNA" benchmarks from UK warehouses to continuously improve the platform's predictive models without any facility sharing sensitive commercial data. For example, efficiency patterns from high-performing Midlands fulfilment centres can help optimise audit strategies for new Northern Powerhouse facilities. This network effect creates collective intelligence that grows more powerful as more warehouses join.
